Global Warming

The Earth is our home, and we need to take care of it. Learn about the causes and effects of global warming and what you can do to help.

What is Global Warming?

Global warming is the long-term heating of Earth's climate system due to human activities, primarily the burning of fossil fuels such as coal, oil, and gas, which releases large amounts of greenhouse gases into the atmosphere.

Causes of Global Warming

Deforestation

Deforestation, or the removal of forests, is one of the main causes of global warming. Trees absorb carbon dioxide from the atmosphere, so when forests are destroyed, the carbon stored in the trees is released into the atmosphere, contributing to the greenhouse effect.

Transportation

The transportation sector is a major contributor to global warming. Cars, trucks, and other vehicles produce greenhouse gases such as carbon dioxide, which trap heat in the atmosphere and cause the Earth's temperature to rise.

Effects of Global Warming

  • Rising sea levels and coastal flooding
  • Increased frequency and severity of heat waves
  • More intense storms and hurricanes
  • More frequent and severe wildfires
  • Loss of biodiversity
